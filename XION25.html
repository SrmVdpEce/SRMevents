ABOUT ROBOTICS CLUB

The Robotics Club of SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Vadapalani has been conducting various activities related to the field of Robotics like WORKSHOPS, TECHNICAL COMPETITIONS, and OUTREACH ACTIVITIES. The club aims to train our young minds in the latest field of Robotics and encourages students from all branches of Engineering. At present, the club has more than 850 student members from SRM Vadapalani.

Since its inception in March 2011, the club has conducted numerous workshops on advanced robot designs using Arduino and PIC microcontrollers. Students have gained hands-on experience in building gesture-controlled, mind-controlled, underwater robots, quadcopters, and biped robots. The best teams are selected to participate in the National Robotics Competition (NRC) at IIT Bombay.

Another major activity of this club is the national-level Techfest XION. This annual event attracts students from all over India and features competitions like Robo Soccer, Pick and Place robots, line-follower robots, and technical paper presentations. Eminent scientists from organizations like DRDO, CVDRE, NIOT, and Nokia have addressed these events. The club also conducts outreach activities to teach robotics skills to school students.
